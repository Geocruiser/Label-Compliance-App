# Malt Beverage TTB Label Rules (Draft)

These draft rules are based on:
- `malt-beverage-labeling-checklist-information.pdf`
- 27 CFR part 7
- 27 CFR part 16
- 27 CFR part 25 (where cross-referenced)

Use these as implementation rules for label review workflows. This is not legal advice and does not replace full regulatory review.

## Scope

- Applies to malt beverage labels requiring COLA review.
- Mandatory information must comply with all applicable provisions in 27 CFR parts 7, 16, and 25.

## Rule Set

### MB-01: Brand Name
- Label must include brand name under which product is sold.
- Brand name must match the COLA application brand-name entry.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.64.

### MB-02: Designation (Class/Type or Other Designation)
- Label must include either:
  - Recognized class/type designation, or
  - Distinctive/fanciful name together with a truthful and adequate statement of composition.
- Designation must be separate and apart from other information and free of conflicts.
- If formula-required, formula must be approved and statement of composition must match (or be more specific than) formula labeling instructions.
- Where used, fanciful name must match application entry.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.63; 27 CFR part 7, subpart I; 27 CFR 7.52(b).

### MB-03: Name and Address (Domestic Product)
- For products wholly fermented in the United States, label must include bottler name and address (city/state) consistent with brewer records/application.
- Name/address may appear on label or be blown/embossed/molded into container.
- Name/address must be shown without intervening text and use appropriate bottler identity practices.
- If principal place of business or multiple brewery locations are used, presentation must not mislead and must satisfy location coding/notification requirements where applicable.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.66; 27 CFR 25.141; 27 CFR 25.142.

### MB-04: Net Contents
- Net contents must appear on label or container marking.
- Statement must use U.S. customary units and acceptable abbreviations for volume.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.70.

### MB-05: Alcohol Content / Alcohol by Weight (Conditional)
- Alcohol content statement is mandatory only where required (for example, alcohol contributed by added non-beverage ingredients containing alcohol, excluding hop extract, or where state law requires).
- If alcohol by weight is stated, it must appear with ABV statement and be accurately converted.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.65.

### MB-06: Government Health Warning
- Label must include exact warning statement required by regulation.
- Formatting and wording checks:
  - `GOVERNMENT WARNING` in capitals and bold
  - Correct punctuation/capitalization (including Surgeon General)
  - Presented as one statement, separate and apart from other information
- Citation: 27 CFR part 16.

### MB-07: Country of Origin (Imported Product Only)
- Imported malt beverages must include country-of-origin statement compliant with CBP requirements.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.69; 19 CFR parts 102 and 134.

### MB-08: Name and Address (Imported Product)
- Imported products must include importer name and address (city/state) consistent with permit and COLA application.
- Importer identity must follow an appropriate phrase (for example, `Imported by`) with no intervening text.
- If bottling/production activities occur after importation, labeling path must follow the applicable domestic or imported rules as triggered.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.67-7.68.

### MB-09: FD&C Yellow #5 Disclosure
- If FD&C Yellow #5 is used, label must specifically disclose it.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.63(b)(1).

### MB-10: Cochineal Extract / Carmine Disclosure
- If cochineal extract or carmine is used, label must specifically disclose it.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.63(b)(2).

### MB-11: Sulfite Declaration
- If total sulfur dioxide is 10 ppm or greater, label must declare sulfites.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.63(b)(3).

### MB-12: Aspartame Declaration
- If aspartame is used, label must include:
  - `PHENYLKETONURICS: CONTAINS PHENYLALANINE.`
- Statement must be in all capitals and separate and apart from other information.
- Citation: 27 CFR 7.63(b)(4).
